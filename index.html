About Me

Hi there! I'm Labiba, a front-end developer and UX researcher with a passion for creating intuitive and accessible web experiences. Currently advancing my knowledge in Computer Science at New York Institute of Technology, I blend technical expertise with a user-centered approach to build digital solutions that make a difference.

My journey in tech began at Baruch College, where I earned my BBA in Computer Information Systems. Since then, I've had the opportunity to work with educational institutions to enhance their digital platforms, making them more responsive, accessible, and user-friendly.

I thrive in collaborative environments where I can apply my skills in web development, UX research, and problem-solving. Whether it's writing clean, maintainable code or conducting user research to inform design decisions, I'm committed to creating seamless digital experiences that address real user needs.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring New York City's diverse neighborhoods, and exploring restaurants. I'm always open to connecting with fellow developers and designers, so feel free to reach out!
