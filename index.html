About Me

I’m Labiba, a Product Manager at TD Bank building installment credit and mobile banking products for 10M+ customers across the U.S. I own TD Payment Plans, the bank’s BNPL product, and the TD Mobile & Digital Banking platform across iOS, Android, and web.

My work spans the full product stack: writing user stories, running usability tests, negotiating with credit risk teams, managing CFPB compliance, and shipping features that make payments feel simpler. I’ve worked across competitive analysis, roadmap strategy, regulatory compliance, UX design, and engineering delivery to get here.

I also build things on the side. Most recently Maintainly, a property management tool I took from idea to monetized MVP in 10 weeks. Out of the office you’ll find me exploring New York, planning mystery adventures for friends, and always looking for the next thing to build.
